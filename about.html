Our story

Over 30 Years of Excellence in Dallas Landscaping

Our mission is to enhance the beauty and functionality of outdoor spaces in Dallas through expert design, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service.

Richardson Landscape Inc was founded in 1992 by John Richardson with a simple philosophy: treat every yard as if it were your own. Starting with a single truck and a passion for horticulture, John built a reputation for reliability and artistry. Over the decades, the company has grown into a full-service landscaping firm serving hundreds of Dallas homeowners and businesses.

Today, we remain family-owned and operated, with a team of skilled designers, horticulturists, and masons who share John's commitment to quality. We stay current with industry trends and sustainable practices, ensuring our clients receive the best solutions for their landscapes.
