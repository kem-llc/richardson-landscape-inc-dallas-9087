Our story

Dallas's Trusted Landscaping Partner for Over 28 Years

Our mission is to enhance the beauty and value of your property through exceptional landscaping and lawn care, delivered with integrity and craftsmanship.

Richardson Landscape Inc was founded in 1995 by John Richardson with a passion for transforming ordinary yards into extraordinary landscapes. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to quality, John built a reputation for reliability and attention to detail. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service landscaping company serving homeowners and businesses across Dallas.

Today, our team of skilled designers, horticulturists, and craftsmen continues that tradition. We combine creative vision with technical expertise to deliver results that exceed expectations. Whether it's a complete landscape renovation or weekly lawn maintenance, we treat every project with the same dedication that has made us a trusted name in the community.
